The Agenda

SIX PILLARS.
ONE MISSION.

A union built to work for every single student at Great Ife without exception.

01
Welfare & Mental Health

Establish a dedicated Student Wellness Centre, expand free counselling sessions, and create an emergency hardship fund for students in acute need.

02
Academic Excellence

Lobby for a grading transparency portal, structured departmental tutorials, and a formal student-faculty dialogue calendar every semester.

03
Infrastructure & Safety

Advocate for rehabilitated study spaces, functional hostel facilities, and a university-wide emergency response protocol co-managed by the union.

04
Enterprise & Opportunity

Launch the Union Internship Board, a partnership with alumni and corporates to create paid internship pipelines for Great Ife students.

05
Inclusion & Representation

Ensure every faculty, minority group, and special-needs student has a direct channel into union decision-making. No voice left behind.

06
Transparency & Accountability

Monthly activity reports, open budgets, and quarterly congress. Leadership with nothing to hide and everything to prove.

"THE BEST FORM OF REPRESENTATION ISN'T ENTIRELY THE ONE THAT SOLVES PROBLEMS, IT'S THE ONE THAT PREDICTS THEM BEFORE IT HAPPENS."
— Arogs · #RiseWithIMPACT

01
Great Ife SU Career Hub

Nigeria's unemployment crisis is not caused by your degree, it is caused by the failure to connect talent to opportunity. A staffed, resourced Union Career Hub will operate every semester: internship pipelines, CV clinics, industry linkages, and alumni networks activated for every student, every faculty. Not a noticeboard. A living bridge from Ile-Ife to the world.

02
Dedicated Internship Office

A digital Union Internship Hub — resourced and actively connecting students to Intership opportunities every semester.

03
Open Budget Policy

All union financial allocations published monthly. Every naira accounted for. Students can submit queries and receive documented responses within 7 days.

04
Quarterly Congress

Compulsory Congress where the union leadership faces unfiltered student questions and responds with concrete action plans.

05
Mental Health Integration

Partner with the appriopriate university body to embed mental health services into student life with stigma-reduction campaigns and structured peer support training.

06
Active Representation of Students at all Levels

Ensuring every student voice is heard and represented at departmental, faculty, university, and external stakeholder levels through consistent advocacy, engagement, and accountability.

07
Union Digital Platform

A unified student portal for union services, schorlaship schemes, petition submission, event registration, and feedback — so you never chase a union official to be heard.
